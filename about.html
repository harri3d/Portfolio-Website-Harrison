about harrison

Little is known about this mysterious individual. Rumors circulate that he graduated from GNOMON SCHOOL OF VISUAL EFFECTS with a concentration in SIMULATION ART. Has an uncontrollable compulsion to make THINGS THAT DON'T EXIST appear like they do.

His work is grounded in a deep fascination with the physical world: he's the kind of person who studies WATER a little too closely, thinks about the behavior of FIRE more than most, and has an arguably unreasonable appreciation for LIGHT REFRACTION.

A background in TRADITIONAL DRAWING and PAINTING informs everything he makes, with inspiration drawn from SURREAL and FANTASY ART.

When he's not at his desk, he can be found on the trails of HUMBOLDT COUNTY, CAMERA in hand — gathering reference, chasing light, and possibly returning with better ideas than the ones he came with.
