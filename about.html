about harrison

Little is known about this mysterious individual. Rumors circulate that he graduated from GNOMON SCHOOL OF VISUAL EFFECTS with a concentration in SIMULATION ART.

Experts confirm he possesses an uncontrollable compulsion to make THINGS THAT DON'T EXIST appear like they do. Symptoms include staring at WATER for too long, narrating the physics of things that are on FIRE, and knowing more about LIGHT REFRACTION than is reasonable.

Unverified sources suggest he comes bearing a background in TRADITIONAL DRAWING and PAINTING, and draws inspiration from SURREAL and FANTASY ART.

In his natural habitat — the trails of HUMBOLDT COUNTY — he has been spotted carrying a CAMERA and quietly collecting better ideas than the ones he arrived with. Whether this is a creative practice or simply a complicated excuse to go outside remains under investigation.
