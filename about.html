About Me - Hi!

Based in Los Angeles * Studying at Gnomon School of Visual Effects * Graduating Summer 2025

I'm drawn to VFX because it's like the mad science of the art world. There's the power to slightly change one parameter and redefine the laws of physics, watching your experiment unfold like some sort of Rube Goldberg machine. How could that ever get boring?

I have a background in traditional drawing and painting, and an AS in Graphic Design. Design principles are really handy when it comes to art directing chaotic simulations.

I get super inspired from surreal and fantasy art and animation. I like things that aren't afraid to be weird. Beyond my professional interests, I enjoy exploring LA's nature trails and photography, which provides me with better ideas.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me! Feel free to get in touch, I'm looking forward to hearing from you.

- Harrison
