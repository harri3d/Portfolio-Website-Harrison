Cannon-Bomb

It's a cannonball, it's a bomb. Need I say more?

Every FX reel needs an explosion. Since I had to do it, I wondered, how can I do it in an interesting way? Inspired by the slapstick humour of the cartoons I grew up with, I originally imagined something heavy crashing into a pile of conveniently located TNT. If I try to make the animation into a visual joke, it forces me to examine my ideas and make them as clear as possible.

Eventually I realized I could combine my ideas into a cartoon bomb, the most immediately recognizable explosive! I figured a pirate theme would fit right in, and would also give me an opportunity to flex my RBD simulation skills, as the explosion could strew around all the pirate treasure.
