Cannon-Bomb

It's a cannonball, it's a bomb. Need I say more?

Project Breakdown

Every FX reel needs an explosion. I wondered, how can I do it differently?

My idea for this project was inspired by classic cartoon violence. I thought it would be a good opportunity to make something funny and surprising. I decided to exaggerate a few elements but otherwise stick to realism.

The first priority was to make a simple pre-visualization to figure out the composition, camera movement and timing. The cameras reacts to the rocking of the ship and the explosion in a cartoony way.
