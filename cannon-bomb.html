Cannon-Bomb

It's a cannonball, it's a bomb. Need I say more?

Project Breakdown

Every FX reel needs an explosion. How can I do it differently?

The inspiration is classic cartoon violence, a good opportunity to make something funny and surprising. I decide to exaggerate a few elements but otherwise stick to realism.

I animate a basic pre-viz to experiment with composition, camera movement and timing. In classic cartoon fashion, the camera shudders at the explosion. KA-BLAM!

Thinking about themes to use, a pirate ship seems like a good fit. Scattering all the pirate treasure could showcase my RBD skills. As a bonus, I get to do slow-mo of shiny things.
